Research interests

My research interests lie in analysis of partial differential equations and spectral theory. I am also interested in problems from differential geometry and mathematical physics.

We are currently investigating Maxwell's equations on half-spaces with biperiodic boundaries, both from perturbation-theoretic and spectral-theoretic viewpoints. My previous work is related to spectral properties of Maxwell's operator (basically, curl) on 3-tori endowed with trigonometric Hermitian metrics.
